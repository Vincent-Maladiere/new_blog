# 4. Statistics

## 4.1 Intro

We estimate probability models parameters $\theta$ from data $\mathcal{D}$. Most methods are optimizations of the form:

$$
\begin{equation}
\hat{\theta}=\argmin_{\theta} \mathcal{L}(\theta)
\end{equation}
$$
